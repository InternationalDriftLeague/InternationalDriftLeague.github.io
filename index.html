  • WHAT ARE PROFESSIONAL DRIFT COMPETITIONS?

    As with any sport, casual drifters quickly reached a level where they wanted to test their skills against other drivers in a competition format. Professional level drifting competitions are hugely exhilarating and will often feature some of the most exciting driving you’re ever likely to see.

About

Pilots from all over the world

We have pilots from Russia, Germany, America, Philipines, Italy, England and all of them in one place.

Custom maps

We create unique configurations on tracks that are loved by many, and we use custom 3d models for similarity with real championships.

WELCOME

We are a young made series whose history started with a jokes between two teams BLUM and Falken. We teamed up and organized our first ever event which was one of the best events we did. We found our way to make top class competitions and many drivers from around the world liked it. The new unique stream graphics ,energetic commentators , close battles, you wont get bored.
